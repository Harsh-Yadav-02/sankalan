Turn-Coat

SANKALAN DUCS 23 💥brings forth yet another edition of the most popular and claimed to be one of the best events - TURN COAT 🎙️🔥.

We welcome young minds, both extroverts and introverts to put forth their opinions over topics chosen from an extremely diverse spectrum of our day to day lives and more.

Have opinions, express yourself, and then challenge yourself 💪 and switch sides to contradict your own self 😎.

Let Hypocrisy be awarded for once!

Register yourself now!

RULES

  1. The event comprises multiple rounds.
  2. You will be given 1 minute to think about the topic you chose.
  3. Speak either in favour of or against the topic till the bell rings.
  4. After the bell rings, speak in the opposite of the motion you chose earlier.
  5. Decision of the organizers shall be final and binding.

Duration

2 hours
So get ready to challenge yourself and put your A-game forward! 😌💯
