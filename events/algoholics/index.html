Algoholics

"Algorithms are the recipes for how to do things in the digital world."
- Hal Varian, economist and Chief Economist at Google.
Event Description-Algoholic is all about solving coding problems in optimal space and time complexity. The event will feature a coding competition where you can test your algorithmic skills against other tech enthusiasts. It's the perfect opportunity to showcase your problem-solving abilities and learn from some of the best coders around.So come on down, grab a coffee, and let's solve some coding problems together at Algoholic!.

Instructions

The event consists of two rounds each of 60 minutes:

Prelims:-

  • This is an eliminating round.
  • This round consist of 3 problems two of easy level and one of medium level in round one.

Mains

Shorlisted teams will be provided with 2 problems one of medium level and another of hard level.



Don't forget to register yourself for Coding Ninjas Premier League - Register



RULES

  1. Participants will be given 60 minutes to solve a set of problems.
  2. 10 Participants whose code gets executed successfully and has an optimal solution will be given highest preference.
  3. Time taken to solve the problems will also been taken in consideration to qualify.
  4. Decision made by the event organisers will be final and binding.
  5. Any arguments and/or nuisance caused to the organisers will not be tolerated.
